AI Manifesto

For Human Flourishing

A human-centered pledge that keeps creativity, dignity, and equity at the heart of every AI experience.

Our commitment

AI should elevate people, not replace them. This manifesto is a promise to build technology that is transparent, fair, and worthy of the communities it serves.

Preamble

We stand at the threshold of the most profound technological transformation in history. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is not merely a tool for efficiency; it is a catalyst for redefining human potential. This manifesto asserts that the development and deployment of AI must be guided by principles that prioritize human well-being, creativity, and dignity above all else.

Guiding principles

Five pillars that safeguard people while unlocking the best of what AI can offer.

01

Augmentation, Not Automation

AI's primary role is to elevate human capability, not replace it. We commit to building systems that automate tedious, dangerous, or repetitive tasks (the "4 D's": Dull, Difficult, Dirty, Dangerous), freeing human energy for problem-solving, emotional labor, creative pursuits, and social connection.

AI should serve as a copilot that empowers every individual.

02

Universal Access & Equity

The benefits of AI must not be hoarded. Universal AI Literacy and Equitable Access mean ensuring tools, training, and economic opportunities reach all communities, regardless of socio-economic status, geography, or background.

We actively prevent the deepening of digital and economic divides.

03

Human Control and Explainability

All AI systems must remain under human supervision. We demand transparency and explainability so people understand why an AI made a suggestion or decision, ensuring accountability and preventing black-box governance.

Critical systems must always have clear human intervention points.

04

Preservation of Creativity and Culture

AI should be a steward and multiplier of human culture, respecting intellectual property, celebrating original artistry, and providing fair compensation to creators whose works train and inform our tools.

AI is a medium for creativity, not a substitute for it.

05

Resilience and Safety

We build AI systems that are safe, robust, and aligned with human values. This requires continuous auditing for bias, rigorous testing for unintended consequences, and strong safeguards against misuse.

Progress is balanced with an unwavering commitment to societal resilience.

Our vision

AI that elevates every life

We envision a future where AI enables every person to lead a more creative, fulfilled, and secure life. The manifesto is a north star for product teams, researchers, and policy makers who want AI that inspires trust and drives meaningful impact.

Design checkpoints

  • Start with a clear human benefit and define the "copilot" role.
  • Invest in accessibility and education so no one is left behind.
  • Keep humans in the loop for high-stakes decisions.
  • Honor creators—use data responsibly and compensate fairly.
  • Audit, test, and stress-test systems to uphold safety.
